# Discord Word Scramble Bot

Word scramble games are a fun way to keep Discord communities active. Players compete to solve scrambled words and earn rewards.

XponentBot includes an endless word scramble system designed to create quick and engaging challenges throughout the day.

## Features

* Unlimited word challenges
* Fast-paced gameplay
* Community engagement
* Reward integration
* Easy setup

Word games are particularly effective because they encourage repeated participation while remaining simple and accessible.
